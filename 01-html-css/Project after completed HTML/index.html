Taco Trivia

When Tacos first appeared in the US?

Tacos first appeared in the United States in the early 1900 s, around the same time Mexican migrants began arriving for work on railroads and in mines. These early tacos were primarily associated with Mexican laborers and were considered a lower-class street food, often sold by women in street food stalls or taquerias. (Source Twisted Taco )
